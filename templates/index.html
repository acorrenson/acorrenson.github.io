About Me

  • Email : arthur.correnson@ens-rennes.fr
  • GitHub : acorrenson
  • LinkedIn : Arthur Correnson

I am a PhD student at CISPA and Saarland University under the supervision of Bernd Finkbeiner. My current research focuses on the formal verification of static analyzers and compilers using the Coq proof assistant. More generally, I am interested in all aspects of programming languages and formal verification.

Exciting news!

April 2023
I am co-advising the bachelor thesis of Iona Kuhn on the formalization of omega regular expressions in Coq

Research Interests

  • Formally verified compilation/static analysis
  • Certified constraint solvers
  • Formalization of floating-point arithmetic
  • Quantum computing

Teaching

  • (2022-2023) teaching assistant for Automata, Games and Verification
  • (2021-2022) tutor for Automated Reasoning

Experiences in the Industry

From 2022 to 2023, I was an employee at AbsInt GmbH working on the CompCert compiler.

Other Activities

Aside from research and studies, I dedicate my free-time to the popularization of theoretical computer science. I co-founded the student organization CodeAnon that organises conferences, tutorials and workshops for undergrad students at University Toulouse III.

Awards

I was deeply honored to receive the first prize of ICFP Student Research Competition 2022 in the graduate category. I received this award for my work on the Formal Verification of a Lazy Abstraction Model Checker supervised by Bernd Finkbeiner.
